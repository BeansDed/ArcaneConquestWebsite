Protagonists

Hero

A brave warrior equipped with a sword and shield, known for their unmatched courage and leadership.

Archer

An expert marksman with a keen eye, the Archer excels at long-range combat and stealth tactics.

Rogue

A master of stealth and agility, the Rogue moves unseen and strikes when least expected.

Healer

The backbone of any party, the Healer wields restorative magic to keep allies alive in battle.

Antagonists

Necromancer

A dark sorcerer who wields forbidden magic, capable of summoning undead minions to do their bidding.

Orc Brute

A powerful and fearsome warrior, the Orc Brute is known for their brute strength and unyielding ferocity.

Dragon

A legendary beast of immense power, the Dragon is the ultimate test of any hero's strength and courage.
